\section{Related Work}
Existing literature on university timetabling typically classifies the problem as a variation of the Resource-Constrained Project Scheduling Problem (RCPSP). Most solutions rely on local search metaheuristics or Constraint Satisfaction Problems (CSP) solvers. While effective for institutional master schedules, these often overlook the "personalized" layer where individual student convenience is the primary objective function.
