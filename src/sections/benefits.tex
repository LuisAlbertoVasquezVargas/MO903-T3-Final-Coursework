\section{Expected Benefits and Challenges}
The project aims to deliver the following expected contributions:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Scientific Contribution:} A novel framework demonstrating the efficiency of hybrid AI-search models for NP-complete scheduling problems.
    \item \textbf{Social and Institutional Impact:} Reduction of the manual workload for students during enrollment and lower server stress for universities during peak usage.
    \item \textbf{Personalization:} Improved academic experience by providing students with schedules that better accommodate their work-study balance.
\end{itemize}

The main challenges anticipated during the project include:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Computational Complexity:} Managing the memory overhead required by the neural network during the real-time pruning of massive search trees.
    \item \textbf{Data Quality:} Ensuring that the synthetic or anonymized training data accurately reflects the diverse and often conflicting preferences of a real student body.
\end{itemize}
